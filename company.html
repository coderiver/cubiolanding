Our Mission

Cubits imagines a world where Bitcoin is accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our mission is to provide everyone with convenient access to Bitcoin. Cubits lays the infrastructure for the rest of the world to speak one common payment language.

Our Business

Founded in 2014, Cubits is Europe's all-inclusive platform to buy, sell, manage, and use Bitcoin. With locations in London and Berlin, our mission is to make Bitcoin easily accessible to everyone around the world. At the end of October 2014, Cubits launched with a Bitcoin wallet and merchant services.
